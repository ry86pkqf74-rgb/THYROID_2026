"Sliding Scale Insulin" (diabetes management), NOT "Surgical Site Infection".

RULES:
1. FALSE POSITIVE — SSI = Sliding Scale Insulin:
   If evidence_span = "SSI" AND context contains:
   "per protocol", "before meals", "for glucose", "insulin", "units", "BG",
   "blood sugar", "novolog", "humalog", "basal", "BG-100/40"
   → FALSE_POSITIVE

2. FALSE POSITIVE — Consent:
   "risk of wound infection" in consent list → FALSE_POSITIVE

3. TRUE POSITIVE — Thyroid surgical site infection:
   "wound infection requiring incision and drainage"
   "wound vac", "negative pressure wound therapy"
   "complicated by wound infection" (in context of thyroid surgery)
   Chief Complaint: "Wound Infection" in ED note
